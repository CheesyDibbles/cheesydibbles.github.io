Teen Murdered In Vacant Lot

Today morning a teen known as Bob Sheldon was stabbed multiple times in a vacant lot. He was dead upon arrival of the scene. Officers suspect Johnny Cade and Ponyboy Michael Curtis
for this murder, as security cameras show two boys, one with dark hair, and the other with light hair. Police interviewed Darrel Curtis, Ponyboys elder brother.

"He ran away early this morning after something happened. I don't think Ponyboy or Johnny murdered Bob. It was just a lucky coincidence that both of those things happened
at the same time."

Police also interviewed Johnnys parents.

"Good for him, atleast he'll learn something about bein' out in the streets. If he gets the electric
chair then what can we do. He was always a pest in the house, all cause of those greasers."

Police got an anonymous tip that the boys are in Texas, tomorrow morning
a unit will go out looking for them.




Church Fire Almost Ends In Fatality

Over the weekend a church caught on fire in Windrixville. The source of the fire is believed to be a lit cigarette but it is yet to be confirmed by the fire department.
Five kids were in the church whilst it being on flame, but two boys dove in and saved the kids. Ponyboy Michael Curtis and Johnny Cade are to thank for this heroic act. Unfortunately both boys were
sent to the hospital. Jerry Wood, a teacher and a witness said this:

"We were having a picnic and all of a sudden the church beside us caught on fire. We heard faint screams coming from inside of the
inside of the church. Before I could do anything, two boys ran in through a broken window and started throwing the kids out. One boy, Ponyboy, made it out safely, but the other, Johnny, came out with
a busted back. I don't think Johnny's going to live, but if he does, he'll be disabled his whole life."

Both boys were transported to the Tulsa General Hospital by an ambulance escort.




Local Hero Dies Of Injuries

Johnny Cade, a hero who saved five kids lives, died in the hospital earlier. A few days ago, Johnny Cade and Ponyboy Michael Curtis saved the lives of five chiildren
from a burning church in Windrixville. Unfortunately, when Johnny tried to exit the church, a long bar of timber struck him in the back. This resulted in him staying in the hospital. Today
he passed away from his injuries. Even if he recovered, he would be paraplegic. Ponyboy, the other boy who saved the kids said this:

"In my head, I knew he was going to die. But even if he didn't die, he would be paralyzed. That may have been worse, being crippled for life. Before he died he told me to stay gold.
It's funny because I told him that poem, "Nothing Gold Can Stay" by Robert Frost. Johnny was my best buddy, we were a tag-team, we did everything together. The greasers was his only family.
His parents abused him and didn't even care if he came home at night. Johnny had such a big influence on the gang, he was basically our pet."

Johnny will be having a funeral on Sunday.


Teen Shot Dead In Police Shootout

Yesterday night there was a police shootout in a vacant lot which resulted in the death of 17 year old Dallas Winston. Police say that he held up a empty gun at
them signaling that he was going to shoot. Tim Bones, a police officer and first-hand witness to the incident had some words to say.

"We had him at gunpoint, but he pulled up a gun and aimed it directly at us. We had no choice but to shoot."

Some reporters say Dallas intentionally wanted to get shot due to his best friends death the same day. Another first-hand witness, Ponyboy Michael Curtis said
this:

"It's not fair, I lost two friends in one night. Dally robbed a grocery store and he was on the run from the cops, we went to go hide him, but it was too late. The cops were already behind him
and within a split second he pulled out his heater and pointed it at the cops. He died before he hit the ground. I still remember him flopping to the ground, under that street lamp. Not soon after
I passed out."

Dally will be having a funeral alongside Johnny Cade, another gang member, on Sunday.
